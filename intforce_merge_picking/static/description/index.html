Merge Multiple Delivery Orders / Incoming Shipments

When there are lots of picking delivery / incoming shipment/receipts it is very hard to manage them as it is difficult for warehouse person to open each delivery and shipment/receipts and process each one of them one by one. Here we are presenting our app which can reduce the time of work of warehouse user who can merge the delivery orders and incoming shipment/receipts .

Select more than one orders to merge the picking with same customers and merge picking through wizard



After clicking on action "merge picking" wizard will open



Once clicked on "Merge Pickings" button new picking will be created with combination of picking and with different number and you can track it as Source Document.





Trying to merge with "Done" status but it will not allow to merge and give you warning.





If Single Picking selected, Merging will not allow.



If Picking Partners are different , Merging will not allow.



If Picking States are different , Merging will not allow.



If Picking Types are different , Merging will not allow.
